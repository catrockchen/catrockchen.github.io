Yuan Yuan and the Hollow Monster

Password: 401B

Yuan Yuan and the Hollow Monster became a Semi-finalist for the Student Academy Awards in 2020. It proceeded to win awards at festivals, and exhibit at more than 30 others. This animated film has been:

  • The Winner of Best Animation for Kids at the Animation Block Party in NYC, the largest animation festival in the east coast. 

  • Publicized on Animation Magazine online.

  • Featured in The New York Times on June 3rd, in their ‘5 things to do this weekend’ segment. 

  • Exhibited at CAAMFest 2021, the nation’s largest festival for Asian and Asian American films. 

  • Reviewed as “a powerful and touching animated film that really moved our committee members. It's well done and we think will resonate well with our audiences (of all ages).”
