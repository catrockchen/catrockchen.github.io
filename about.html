CATHERINE CHEN

TV Writer | Voice Actor

Catherine is a TV writer and voice-actor for kids and family animation.

She is currently working at Paramount Animation on Tiger's Apprentice as production assistant for story, script, and editorial.

For fun, she draws on Instagram. She loves her pets and even made a book about them. Occasionally, she illustrates for childrens books as well.
