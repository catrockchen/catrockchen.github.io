Catherine Chen

Writer-Director | Filmmaker

Catherine Chen is a multi-award-winning animation filmmaker based in Los Angeles, CA and West Palm Beach, FL. Upon graduating from USC's School of Cinematic Arts, she became a Semi-finalist for the Student Academy Awards with her film, Yuan Yuan and the Hollow Monster.

Catherine is also a screenwriter and storyboard artist. She develops TV shows and started her own animation and IP development studio, Yuan Yuan Animation.

Her favorite part of the process is developing projects and working with her team to make it happen.
